Zolo - Accessibility Statement

Version: v1.0
Last updated: December 2025

1. Our commitment

Zolo is committed to making its website and Android app accessible to the widest possible audience, including people with disabilities.

2. Standards and level of accessibility

The Zolo marketing site is designed to comply with the requirements of Israeli Standard 5568, which is based on the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.0, Level AA.

Where feasible without significant changes to the existing design, we have also implemented relevant requirements from WCAG 2.1 Level AA (for example support for users who prefer reduced motion).

3. Scope of this statement

This statement covers the public pages of the Zolo website, including the English and Hebrew landing pages, legal pages (terms of service, privacy policy, account deletion information) and this accessibility page.

4. Measures taken

Examples of measures taken to improve accessibility on this site include:

  • Use of semantic landmarks such as header, main, and footer.
  • Clear heading hierarchy with a single main heading (h1) per page.
  • A ‚ÄúSkip to main content‚Äù link for keyboard users.
  • Keyboard operable language and theme toggles with accessible labels.
  • Visible focus indicators for all links and buttons.
  • Support for users who prefer reduced motion where possible.

5. Testing

The website has been tested using keyboard-only navigation, common desktop browsers, and automated accessibility tools (such as browser developer tools and axe-based checks) against WCAG 2.0 Level AA and selected WCAG 2.1 Level AA criteria.

6. Known limitations

We are not aware of major accessibility barriers on this marketing site. However, some components (such as marketing screenshots of the app UI) are primarily visual and may not convey the full in-app experience to users of assistive technologies. We aim to provide equivalent text descriptions where practical.

7. Contact and feedback

If you encounter any accessibility difficulty on the site or the app, please contact us at: support@zolo-deals.com.

When contacting us, please include as many details as possible, such as the page address, a description of the problem, and the browser or assistive technology you are using.

8. Requesting information in an alternative format

If you need information from this site in an alternative accessible format (such as plain text email), please send a request to: support@zolo-deals.com.

We will do our best to provide the requested material in an accessible format as soon as reasonably possible.
